U.S. Navy Themed Cremation Urn

This personalized cremation urn is a tribute that honors tradition with a modern, elegant touch. Designed with a U.S. Navy theme, it features striking black edges and a vintage style that makes it a standout addition to any home or memorial space. The exquisite cherry wood construction is enhanced by a premium lacquer finish that preserves its natural beauty and adds a touch of sophistication.

Crafted with attention to detail, the urn includes a customizable nameplate that can be engraved with four lines of text, allowing you to create a deeply personal memorial. It is available in three stylish color options, giving you the freedom to choose a design that best represents your loved one's personality and legacy. The generous 213 cubic inch capacity is suitable for a person up to 205 pounds, making it a practical and meaningful choice for families.

Whether placed in a living room, hallway, or dedicated memorial area, this urn is built to last. It features a secure bottom panel that requires four screws for stability, along with four corner pads that protect your surfaces. The combination of classic craftsmanship and contemporary design ensures both elegance and durability for long-term use and display.
