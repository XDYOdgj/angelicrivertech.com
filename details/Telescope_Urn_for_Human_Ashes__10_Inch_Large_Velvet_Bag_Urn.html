Telescope Urn for Ashes – A Unique and Meaningful Memorial

Celebrate the life of your loved one with this beautifully crafted telescope urn, designed to honor memories with grace and dignity.

This handcrafted telescope urn is an elegant tribute to a departed loved one. Its classic design offers a unique way to display ashes in a home or memorial setting, making it a perfect choice for families who value both beauty and symbolism.

Constructed from durable aluminum, this urn is built to last, offering strength and resilience for both display and transport. The sturdy build ensures that it remains a lasting keepsake for years to come.

Featuring a 10-inch height and 7.5-inch width, this urn is designed for adult ashes and serves as a beautiful centerpiece for both home and burial ceremonies. It holds up to 200 cubic inches of ashes, providing a practical and meaningful option for every family.
