Elegant Botticino Beige Cremation Urn

A timeless tribute to your loved one, this beautifully crafted urn is designed to honor and preserve memories with grace and beauty.

Made from high-quality Botticino Beige marble, it captures a natural, enduring appearance that symbolizes lasting legacy. The urn is carefully designed to hold up to 240 cubic inches of ashes, making it ideal for adult-sized remains.

Every piece is handcrafted in the USA with precision and care, ensuring unmatched durability and a refined aesthetic. Whether placed in a living room, study, or memorial space, this urn complements any home decor with its classic, dignified design.

  • Felt-lined interior to protect furniture and ensure safe placement
