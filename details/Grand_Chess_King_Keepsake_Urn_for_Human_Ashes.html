Grand Chess King Keepsake Urn

A unique and elegant way to honor your loved one's memory. This urn is inspired by the powerful symbol of the chess king, representing the central role your loved one played in your life. Designed to stand out, it blends art and remembrance in a meaningful manner.

Standing at 15 inches tall with a 7-inch base diameter, this urn is spacious enough to hold the ashes of an adult. Each piece is meticulously handcrafted, ensuring a high-quality finish that reflects both care and sophistication. Choose from five beautiful enamel options to customize your tribute to your loved one's personality and style.

Why Choose This Urn?

Featuring a secure bottom closure for safe and peaceful storage of ashes, this urn is both functional and beautiful. The addition of a decorative bow tie offers a personalized touch, aligning with your loved one's individuality. More than just a keepsake, it doubles as a stunning home decorative piece, adding a unique element to any room.

  • Display in living rooms, studies, or any meaningful space
  • Those who appreciate unique design and craftsmanship
  • Anyone looking for a lasting symbol of love and remembrance
