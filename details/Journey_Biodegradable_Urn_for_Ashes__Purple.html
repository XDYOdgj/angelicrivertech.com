Eco-Friendly Biodegradable Cremation Urn

For those who seek a meaningful and sustainable tribute, this biodegradable cremation urn is a thoughtful choice. Made from natural handmade paper, it carries a unique flecked texture that enhances its aesthetic appeal and creates a personal connection to your loved one's legacy.

Designed to honor nature, this urn gradually dissolves in water, allowing ashes to be released back into the environment in an environmentally friendly manner. The included water-soluble bag simplifies the process, making it easy to prepare for a burial that aligns with your values.

Why Choose This Urn?

  • Eco-Conscious Design: Perfect for a natural burial experience, this urn returns to the earth safely and sustainably.
  • Unique Aesthetic: The elegant, flecked handmade paper adds a personal touch and stands out as a meaningful memorial piece.
  • Robust Capacity: With a 220 cubic inch size, it is ideal for holding ashes up to 220 pounds, making it a practical choice for adult-sized memorials.
  • Versatile for Burial Types: Suitable for water, ground, or sea ceremonies, this urn is built for a dignified and respectful final journey.
  • Sustainable Materials: Every aspect of this urn reflects an environmentally responsible choice, from its creation to its natural decomposition.

This urn offers a seamless way to honor your loved one in harmony with nature, whether you're planning a traditional burial or a more unconventional one in a peaceful setting. Its design focuses on both beauty and sustainability, ensuring your memorial is as respectful as it is meaningful.
